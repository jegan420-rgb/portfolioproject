Project 2: Miskatonic Nature Retreat banner

This page showcases one of my earlier Adobe Animate projects, for a fictitious client called the Miskatonic Tourism Board.

Project Description

In the early 20th-century horror fiction of H.P. Lovecraft, now largely in the public domain, he wrote of fictional places like Arkham, Dunwich, and Innsmouth, all located in a region he called Miskatonic. This place was the backdrop for the cosmic horror tales he penned in the 1920s and 1930s.

My concept for this project was to envision a banner ad for Miskatonic-region tourism that would be inviting. Designed to seem above board, the ad could attract attention and bring visitors in. (Whether they can ever leave, at least in the stories, is another matter entirely.)
