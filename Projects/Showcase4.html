Project 4: Feline Monthly magazine cover

For the final Photoshop course project, I created a magazine cover for the fictitious Feline Monthly magazine.

Project Description

For the third and final project in the Photoshop course, I returned to a subject I wanted to use back in the Animate course, but could never get to work. I wanted to use my dearly-departed cat, Shadow, for this project and made her the cover model for a pet magazine.

The photo of the subject was taken by me in the late 80s or early 90s. The image I worked from is a flatbed scan of a photo with a significant amount of retouching and dust removal needed to complete the project.
